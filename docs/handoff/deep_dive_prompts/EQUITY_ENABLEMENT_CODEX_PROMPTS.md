## EQ-06 — Pre-sizing equity leverage / buying-power guard (Reg-T aware)

**Mission:** Add a runtime equity-leverage / buying-power guard to the portfolio risk engine that bounds gross EQUITY-asset-class leverage against live account equity and buying_power **at sizing time**, mirroring the merged FX-05 leverage stage.
